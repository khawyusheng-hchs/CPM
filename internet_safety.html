  • **DDoS Attacks:**

    Prevention methods: Use a DDoS protection service, configure network firewalls, and implement rate limiting.

  • **Brute Force Attacks:**

    Prevention methods: Use strong, unique passwords, enable multi-factor authentication, and limit login attempts.

  • **SQL Injections:**

    Prevention methods: Validate user input, use parameterized queries, and keep software up-to-date.

  • **Spoofing:**

    Prevention methods: Verify sender addresses, use email authentication protocols, and be cautious of unsolicited messages.

  • **Rainbow Table Attacks:**

    Prevention methods: Use strong, unique passwords, avoid common passwords, and enable password hashing algorithms.

  • **Scams:**

    Prevention methods: Be cautious of unsolicited offers, verify information, and never share personal or financial details with unknown individuals.

Prevention Tips

  • Use strong, unique passwords
  • Keep software updated
  • Be cautious of suspicious emails and links
  • Use antivirus and anti-malware software
  • Enable two-factor authentication
  • Back up your data regularly
  • Be mindful of public Wi-Fi networks
