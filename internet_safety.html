Internet Safety Tips

Common Attacks and Prevention

  • **DDoS Attacks:** Avoid clicking on suspicious links or downloading attachments from unknown sources. Keep your software updated with the latest security patches.
  • **Brute Force Attacks:** Use strong, unique passwords and enable two-factor authentication (2FA) whenever possible.
  • **SQL Injections:** Be cautious about entering data into online forms and avoid clicking on suspicious links.
  • **Spoofing:** Verify the sender's email address and be wary of unexpected emails or attachments.
  • **Rainbow Table Attacks:** Use strong, unique passwords and avoid using easily guessable information.
  • **Scams:** Be skeptical of unsolicited emails, phone calls, or messages asking for personal information or money.

Types of Malware and Prevention

  • **Virus:** Use antivirus software and keep it updated. Avoid downloading files from untrusted sources.
  • **Worms:** Keep your operating system and software up-to-date with security patches.
  • **Trojan Horse:** Be cautious about downloading software from unknown sources and avoid clicking on suspicious links.
  • **Ransomware:** Back up your important files regularly and avoid opening attachments from unknown senders.
  • **Spyware:** Use antivirus software and be cautious about installing new software from unknown sources.
  • **Adware:** Use ad-blocking software and be cautious about clicking on advertisements.
